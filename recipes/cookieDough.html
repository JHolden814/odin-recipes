Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 1/2 cup milk chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips

Directions

  1. To heat-teat your flour so it is safe to use: Place flour in a microwave-safe dish and cook for 1 minute and 15 seconds, stirring it every 15 seconds. Set aside.
  2. Beat sugar and butter with an electric mixer in a large bowl until craemy. Beat in vanilla extract and salt. Add heat-treated flour; mix until a crumbly dough forms. Stir in milk until dough is just combined; fold in milk chocolate chips and mini chocolate chips.
