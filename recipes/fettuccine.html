Ingredients

  • 24 oz dry fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup butter
  • 3/4 pint heavy cream
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 dash garlic salt
  • 3/4 cup grated Romano cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to boil. Add fettuccine and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  2. Melt butter into cream in a large saucepan over low heat; add salt, pepper, and garlic salt. Increae the heat to medium; stir in grated Romano and Parmesan cheese until melted and sauce has thickened.
  3. Add cooked past to sauce and toss until thoroughly coated; serve immediately.
